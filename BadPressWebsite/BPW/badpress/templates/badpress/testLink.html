-Our Mission-


To let voters know what matters to them.

We live in an age where information is everywhere, flooding our feeds and filling our headlines.
Yet for all the information at our fingertips, the average voter feels less informed than ever.
Our goal is to take away the spin, to cut through the smoke and shine a light on the only thing that matters: data.

Using machine learning and intelligent sentiment analysis scripts, we will process the huge swathe of data mired across the internet, and present it to you in one web app.

With the 2018 Senate Election looming in November, now is the time to take a close look at your constituents.

Who deserves your vote?
